Model assumptions

Domestic tariff is applied progressively to grid-import kWh. Solar reduces grid import first; the remaining grid kWh is billed again through the tariff tiers. Annual degradation reduces solar output. O&M is deducted yearly. Tariff escalation and discount rate are user-controlled assumptions.
